Modern Cryptocurrency Exchange Platform

A Secure, Real-time, and User-Friendly Crypto Eco-system


Presented by: Sreya Ch

Overview

  • Unified Platform: Trading, Market Tracking, Wallet Management, and Mining Analytics in one place.
  • User-Centric: Designed for beginners and pros with a focus on ease of use.
  • Real-time Simulation: Provides a realistic experience of the crypto market.
  • Tech Stack: Modern Web Technologies (HTML5, Bootstrap, JS).

The Problem

  • Crypto platforms are often too complex and intimidating for beginners.
  • Fragmented tools: Trading, Learning, and Analytics are usually on different sites.
  • Lack of safe "Sandbox" environments to learn without risking real money.

The Solution: Cryptopay

  • Simplified UI: Clean, Binance-inspired Dark Mode interface.
  • All-in-One: Combines Market, Wallet, and Mining.
  • Educational: Integrated specific "How it Works" and FAQ sections.
  • Safe Learning: Real-time data simulation with dummy balances.

Technology Stack

Frontend

  • HTML5 & CSS3
  • Bootstrap 5 (Responsive)
  • JavaScript (ES6+)
  • Reveal.js (Presentation)

Integrations

  • TradingView Widgets (Live Charts)
  • Chart.js (Analytics)
  • FontAwesome (Icons)
  • LocalStorage (Persistence)

Seamless User Experience

  • Secure Auth: Login & Registration with local session persistence.
  • Dynamic Profile:
    • Real-time data binding (Name, Email).
    • Random UID: Unique 9-digit User ID generation.
    • "Verified" Status Badge.
  • Responsive: Optimized for Mobile/Desktop.

Market & Trading

  • Live Data: Real-time BTC/USDT price charts via TradingView.
  • News Feed: Rolling timeline of global crypto news.
  • Market Dashboard: dedicated page for tracking top coin performance (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether).

Secure Wallet

  • Dashboard: View estimated Total Balance (BTC & USD/INR).
  • Asset Breakdown: Visual split of holdings.
  • Transaction History: Local persistence of Deposits/Withdrawals.
  • Mock Payments: Simulation of UPI/Card payment methods.

Mining Analytics

  • Visual Dashboard: Interactive Chart.js graphs.
  • Key Metrics: Hash Rate, Power Consumption, Efficiency.
  • Admin Tools: Network status monitoring.

Future Scope

  • Backend: Node.js/Python integration for server-side DB.
  • Blockchain: Real on-chain data fetching.
  • P2P Trading: Enable peer-to-peer exchange features.
  • AI Bot: Advanced NLP for trading advice.
