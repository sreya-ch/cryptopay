Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a form of digital money that exists only online and does not have a physical form like coins or notes. It uses cryptography to secure transactions and protect users from fraud. Cryptocurrency works on a decentralized network, which means it is not controlled by any bank or central authority. Because of this, transactions are transparent, secure, and can be made directly between users over the internet. Popular examples of cryptocurrencies include Bitcoin and Ethereum.
